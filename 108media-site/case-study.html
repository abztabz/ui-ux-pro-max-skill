Aura Skincare
Launch

A finished formula and an empty page — to a sold-out launch in nine days. We built the brand, shot the campaign and ran the rollout, end to end. Scroll through, or drag, hover and explore.

ClientAura Skincare
Year2025
Timeline6 weeks to launch
ScopeBrand · Photography · Social · Campaign
The challenge

Three problems, one deadline.

01

A saturated categoryClean beauty is loud and look-alike. Aura needed a point of view, not another minimalist label.

02

No existing audienceZero followers, zero list, zero awareness. Demand had to be manufactured before launch, not after.

03

A six-week runwayBrand, assets, content and campaign all had to ship together — with no room for rework.

The approach

Four phases, one continuous thread.

01 Brand foundations
01

Brand foundations

We set the tone before a single pixel: a calm, confident voice, a porcelain-and-blush palette, and a wordmark with breathing room. The brand had to feel like skincare you trust, not skincare that shouts.

  • Positioning
  • Logo & wordmark
  • Palette & type
  • Guidelines
02

Art direction & photography

A two-day shoot delivered the hero campaign imagery — product, texture and lifestyle — lit soft and warm so every frame felt premium and ownable across web, print and social.

  • Art direction
  • Product photography
  • Lifestyle
  • Retouching
03

Social content system

We turned the shoot into a system — a modular grid, reel templates and motion stings — so the brand could post daily without diluting the look. Consistency on autopilot.

  • Grid system
  • Reel templates
  • Motion stings
  • Calendar
04

Launch campaign

A three-week teaser-to-launch arc across organic and paid, anchored by a fast landing page. Waitlist first, scarcity second, sell-out third.

  • Strategy
  • Paid social
  • Landing page
  • Launch playbook
